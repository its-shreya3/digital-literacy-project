 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

In this task, I created a digital literacy infographic to raise awareness about the importance of digital skills among students.

🔹Tool Used
- Canva (for designing the infographic)

🔹 Topics Covered
- What is Digital Literacy  
- Useful Digital Tools (Google Docs, Canva, GitHub, Grammarly)  
- Safe Internet Practices  
- Email Etiquette  

🔹 Description
The infographic visually explains the concept of digital literacy and highlights essential skills required for students in the digital age. It uses icons, colors, and structured sections to make the information easy to understand and engaging.

🔹 Learning Outcome
Through this task, I learned how to present information visually and effectively using design tools. It also helped me understand the importance of digital awareness and responsible online behavior.

🔹 File
The infographic is available in the `task-1-presentation/` folder.
