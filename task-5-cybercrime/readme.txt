Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention

In this task, I explored cybercrime by analyzing a case study related to UPI (Unified Payments Interface) fraud, which is one of the most common online financial frauds in India.

🔹 Part A – Case Study
I created a case study explaining how UPI fraud occurs, including the method used by attackers, the target users, and the consequences such as financial loss and emotional stress. The case highlights how even aware users can fall victim to realistic scam messages and links.

🔹 Part B – Prevention Checklist
I developed a “Stay Safe Online” checklist containing practical and actionable tips for students. It includes guidelines for general online safety as well as specific measures for financial and UPI security, such as avoiding unknown links, verifying payment requests, and protecting sensitive information.

I also included official reporting channels:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930  

🔹 Learning Outcome
This task helped me understand the seriousness of cybercrime and the importance of awareness, quick action, and safe digital practices. It also encouraged me to adopt better online safety habits.
