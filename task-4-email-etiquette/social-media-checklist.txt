SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS

DOs:
- Use strong and secure passwords
- Keep your profile professional and appropriate
- Verify information before sharing
- Respect others and communicate politely
- Use privacy settings to protect your data

DON’Ts:
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Do not post offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not overshare your location or daily activities
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
