Task 4 – Email Etiquette & Responsible Communication

In this task, I demonstrated professional digital communication skills by drafting two formal emails and creating a social media responsibility checklist.

🔹 Part A – Professional Emails
I wrote two professional emails:
- An email to a professor requesting an assignment deadline extension.
- An email to an internship coordinator expressing interest in a summer internship.

Both emails follow proper email etiquette, including a clear subject line, formal greeting, well-structured body, and appropriate sign-off.

🔹 Part B – Social Media Do’s and Don’ts
I created a checklist highlighting responsible social media behavior for college students. It includes important guidelines such as maintaining professionalism, protecting personal information, and avoiding harmful online activities.

🔹 Learning Outcome
This task helped me understand the importance of clear, respectful, and professional communication in academic and professional environments. It also increased my awareness of responsible social media usage.
