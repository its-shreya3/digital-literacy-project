STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

- Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone (even if they claim to be from a bank)
- Always verify links and avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious URLs
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for added security
- Do not install apps from unknown sources or links

(Financial / UPI Safety)
- Always check the receiver’s name before making any UPI payment
- Do not approve unknown “Collect Requests” on UPI apps
- Regularly check your bank transactions and report any suspicious activity immediately

- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions
- Keep your mobile and apps updated with the latest security updates

(Reporting & Help)
- Report cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number 1930 for immediate assistance
